# UBIRT.AI - App Store Optimization (ASO) Guide

ASO is the process of improving app visibility within the app stores. Higher rankings in the store lead to more downloads and provide powerful "brand authority" signals to Google Search.

## 🎯 1. Keyword Strategy
App store algorithms rely heavily on the **Title** and **Short Description**.

- **Primary Keywords**: AI Motivation, Daily Reels, Sports Performance, Mindset Growth, Productivity App.
- **Secondary Keywords**: Radical Health, Biohacking, Mental Toughness, Success Quotes, Video Motivation.

---

## 📝 2. Metadata Optimization

### A. App Title (Max 30-50 chars)
*Must contain your strongest keyword.*
- **Option 1**: UBIRT.AI: AI Motivation Reels
- **Option 2**: UBIRT.AI: Sports & Mindset AI

### B. Short Description (Max 80 chars)
*This is the most important conversion text.*
> "Master your mindset with AI-powered motivational reels and sports performance."

### C. Long Description (SEO-Focused)
*Include keywords naturally in the first 3 paragraphs.*
> "Elevate your life with UBIRT.AI, the premier AI-driven content hub for motivation and high performance.
> 
> **Why UBIRT.AI?**
> Stop scrolling through noise. UBIRT.AI uses advanced algorithms to deliver high-octane reels across three elite niches:
> 1. **Motivation**: Spark relentless drive with curated mental toughness content.
> 2. **Radical Health**: Optimize your body with biohacking and wellness hacks.
> 3. **Sports Performance**: Master your craft with professional athletic insights.
> 
> Join thousands of elite performers using the 'Daily Spark' to fuel their productivity. Whether you're an athlete or a high-achiever, UBIRT.AI is your digital coach for total transformation."

## 📈 4. Ratings & Reviews Growth
Google Play ranks apps with high engagement and frequent updates.
- **Review Mining**: Use positive reviews as 'testimonials' on your landing page `ubirtai.site`.
- **Keyword Replies**: When replying to reviews, use keywords (e.g., "Glad you like our **AI Motivation** reels!").
