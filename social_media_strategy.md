# UBIRT.AI - Social Media Signal Strategy

Social signals (likes, shares, pins, and mentions) are unofficial ranking factors that tell Google your content is valuable and trending. For an AI Reel platform, social media is your strongest growth engine.

## 📌 1. Pinterest Power (Visual SEO)
Pinterest is a visual search engine, not just social media. It is perfect for "Motivational Quotes" and "Health Tips".

- **Action**: Create a Pinterest Business account indexed as "UBIRT.AI | AI Motivation & Sports Reels".
- **The "Pin-to-Site" Strategy**:
    - **Motivation Pins**: Static images of the "Daily Spark" quotes with the UBIRT.AI logo.
    - **Niche Video Pins**: 7-second muted crops of your premium reels (Movement/Sports).
    - **Link Policy**: Every pin must link directly to its relevant route:
        - `ubirtai.site/motivation`
        - `ubirtai.site/health`
        - `ubirtai.site/sports`

## 🎥 2. The "Short-Form Loop" (TikTok, Reels, Shorts)
Use the 15-second loop strategy to drive app downloads and site visits.

- **Content Pillar**: "How this AI generated Motivation changed my day."
- **Call-to-Action (CTA)**: "Get your Daily Spark at UBIRT.AI (link in bio)."
- **SEO Optimization**: Use keywords in your captions: #AIMotivation #ProductivityHacks #UBIRTAI #SportsPerformance.

## 🐦 3. Twitter (X) - The Tech & Productivity Hub
Twitter is where the "AI Community" lives.

- **The Thread Strategy**: "I built an AI that cures procrastination. Here is how it works..."
- **Engagement**: Reply to big accounts (e.g., Elon Musk, productivity influencers) with meaningful insights and a subtle mention of UBIRT.AI's "Daily Spark".

## 🔗 4. Reddit & Quora (Niche Context)
Drive targeted traffic from people already asking for help.

- **Reddit**:
    - Post in `r/motivation`, `r/productivity`, `r/biohacking`.
    - **Strategy**: Don't just post a link. share a "Daily Spark" text and say "I found this helpful from this AI tool I'm using."
- **Quora**:
    - Answer questions like "How can I stay motivated to work out?" or "Best apps for daily mindset."
    - Link to specialized pages like `ubirtai.site/sports`.
